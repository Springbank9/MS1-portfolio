About

I am an artist from Morayshire who is currently living and working in Edinburgh. This site diplays images of both my artwork and shows a selection of photogrpahs that I have taken. My subject matter is wildlife although I have received commissions for other thins such as portraits of people and buildings. In 2012, I trained at the Florence Academy of Art in Italy for a summer semeter - although I had been drawing for years before then. I undertook lessons in figure painting and academic drawing and learnt how to mix my own paints. While studying art history at Glasgow University, I continued taking commissions including animal portraits, cards and wall drawings. I exhibited at the Game Fair in Scone, Scotland in 2016 in the "Artist in Action" marquee. My most recent skill has been computer programming. With this, I have built my own site (this one) and continue to learn the many ways in which I can expose my creativity online. While I enjoy ary, another major passion is wildlife. I observe it daily wherever I can and continue to sketch, photograph and paint what birds and animals I see in my local environment.
